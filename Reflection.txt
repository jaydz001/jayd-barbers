The Jay’s Barbershop website reflects the NTC mission of building character and being responsive to the needs of the Republic of the Philippines by using technology to help improve customer service and support local businesses. The website gives customers an easy and convenient way to check services, prices, and contact information online, making the business more accessible and modern for today’s generation.

The project also shows creativity, responsibility, and dedication in creating a useful digital platform. Through developing the website, students were able to apply their skills in web design and programming while learning the importance of hard work, teamwork, and problem-solving. By helping a local business improve its online presence, the project contributes to community development and supports the growing use of technology in the Philippines.
